Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 22, 2025

FLZ ("we," "our," or "us") operates feelz.chat. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our service. By using FLZ, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

Account information. When you sign in with Google or email, we collect your email address and, optionally, a display name you provide.

Assessment and journal data. We store the text you submit for analysis, your AI-generated results (including energy, emotion, and clarity dimensions), and any journal entries you write. This is the core content of the service.

Payment information. If you subscribe to FLZ Pro, payments are processed by Stripe. We do not store your credit card number or billing details — Stripe handles this securely. We store your subscription status and a Stripe customer ID.

Usage data. We store a daily assessment count in your browser's local storage to enforce the free tier limit. This is not sent to our servers.

Preference data. Your preferred name and dark mode setting are stored in your browser's local storage on your device only.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the FLZ service — analyzing your check-ins and surfacing insights
  • To process AI analysis of your submitted text using Anthropic's Claude API
  • To display your history, trends, and journal entries
  • To manage your subscription and process payments via Stripe
  • To maintain and improve the service
  • To respond to support requests

We do not sell your personal data. We do not use your data for advertising.

3. AI Processing

When you submit a check-in, the text you enter is sent to Anthropic's API to generate your analysis. Anthropic processes this data to return results and may retain it in accordance with their own privacy policy. We encourage you not to include sensitive identifying information (such as full names of others, financial details, or medical specifics) in your check-ins.

FLZ is a self-reflection tool. The AI analysis it provides is not a substitute for professional mental health care, therapy, or medical advice.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase — database and authentication (data stored on servers in the United States)
  • Anthropic — AI analysis of your check-in text
  • Stripe — payment processing for Pro subscriptions
  • Vercel — web hosting and serverless functions
  • Google — optional sign-in via Google OAuth

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data and entries for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or billing purposes.

6. Data Security

Your data is stored in Supabase with row-level security — you can only access your own entries. Connections are encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data, but no system is completely secure.

7. Children's Privacy

FLZ is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

  • Right to know — you may request details about the personal information we collect and how we use it
  • Right to delete — you may request that we delete your personal information
  • Right to opt out of sale — we do not sell personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination — we will not discriminate against you for exercising these rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

9. Canadian Residents (PIPEDA)

If you are a Canadian resident, you have rights under the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA), including the right to access your personal information and request corrections. Your data is stored on servers in the United States. By using FLZ, you consent to this cross-border transfer.

To make a privacy request, contact us at the email below. We will respond within 30 days.
